Welcome to my homepage. I am currently a Postdoc Scholar in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Decision Analytics, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. My supervisor is Professor Jiheng Zhang.

Prior to joining IEDA, I was a postdoc in the William Davidson Faculty of Industrial Engineering and Management at the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology, Haifa, Israel. I received my Ph.D. degree in June 2015 in Operations Research from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Clear Water Bay, Hong Kong, and B.S. degree in Mathematics from Nanjing University, Nanjing, China.

Research Interest

  • Applications in Service Operations Management, Large Communication Networks.
